Our Survey Specializations

Expert survey services across multiple maritime and technical domains

Cargo Surveys

Comprehensive cargo assessment services ensuring the integrity and condition of goods throughout the transportation process.

  • Damage Surveys: Thorough assessment of damage to soft and hard commodities
  • Lashing & Securing: Professional recommendations and supervision for cargo securing
  • Condition Assessment: Pre-loading and discharge condition evaluation

Nautical Surveys

Specialized nautical survey services covering maritime incidents, environmental issues, and vessel-related assessments.

  • Structural Damage: Assessment of damage to fixed and floating maritime objects
  • Collision Investigation: Comprehensive collision and salvage investigations with supervision
  • Environmental Response: Oil pollution investigations and clean-up supervision

Technical Surveys

Advanced technical survey services covering complex machinery, vessels, and specialized equipment assessments.

  • Hull & Machinery: Comprehensive vessel hull and machinery surveys
  • Aircraft Engines: Specialized aircraft engine assessment and evaluation
  • Mechanical Equipment: Industrial and mechanical equipment surveys
  • Crane Systems: Comprehensive crane and lifting equipment assessment
