About Us

Quality and Policy

The Senior Management of GROUP AGECOSCO is committed to:

  • Reliable Service: Offer inspection and certification services in a reliable, transparent, safe and professional manner.
  • ISO/IEC 17020 Compliance: Meet the requirements of both customers and applicable legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Impartial Performance: Ensure IMPARTIAL and INDEPENDENT performance of the Organization and its members.
  • Competent Staff: Keep inspection staff technically TRAINED and COMPETENT to carry out their activities.
  • Confidentiality: Make CONFIDENTIAL use of all information obtained and generated during the process.
  • Continuous Improvement: Continuously improve our management system to consolidate as a reference for quality services.

Quality Management System

GROUP AGECOSCO has established a comprehensive Quality Management System to ensure consistent delivery of high-standard inspection and certification services across all our operations in West Africa.

Our quality framework is built on the foundation of ISO/IEC 17020 standards, ensuring that every aspect of our service delivery meets international best practices and regulatory requirements.

Key Quality Features:

  • Standardized inspection procedures
  • Qualified and certified personnel
  • Regular quality audits and reviews
  • Continuous improvement processes
