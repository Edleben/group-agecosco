About Us

AGECOSCO is an international marine inspection company, founded in 1999 and accredited to ISO 17020 by the Togo Accreditation Agency. As the only national accreditation body recognized by the Togolese government, we are proud of our affiliation with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Our team of qualified engineers and experts has over 25 years of combined experience in marine inspection. We are committed to ensuring that our assessment bodies comply with national and international standards, providing unparalleled expertise in maritime operations.

Thanks to our wide range of services, including inspection, control and surveillance, GROUP AGECOSCO is your trusted partner for all your maritime operations across West Africa.
