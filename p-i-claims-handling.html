The advantage of sending our in-house surveyors to attend the job rather than subcontracting, is to be much faster, much more technical and to the point, and cheaper.

Our in-house surveyors are highly qualified and experienced professionals, who have been working for us for many years. They are well trained and know the local conditions, the local authorities, the local port and the local shipping companies. They are also well aware of the local customs and practices, which is a great advantage when it comes to dealing with local authorities and shipping companies.

Our claims handlers will instruct the in-house  surveyor to look at all those aspects of the case which will be relevant for the further handling of the case.

All necessary information will be collected and all necessary “official” documents (e.g. Letters of Protest, Letters of Invitations to Joint Survey etc) will be issued on the spot by the in-house surveyor.

Our Approach

We take a proactive approach to P&I claims handling, ensuring that all parties are kept informed throughout the process.

Our team of experts is dedicated to resolving claims efficiently and effectively, minimizing disruption to our clients' operations.

We leverage technology and data analytics to streamline the claims process and provide real-time updates to our clients.

We are committed to continuous improvement and regularly review our processes to ensure we are providing the best possible service.
