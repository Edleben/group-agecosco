Group Agecosco Maritime Company Adopts a systematic approach to health, safety and environmental management, designed to ensure compliance with legislation and continuous improvement in performance. Carries out its activities in such a way as to protect its staff, customers, suppliers and third parties from health hazards/risks. Promote awareness of safe practices, in the belief that all accidents are preventable, and promote individual responsibility for accident prevention. Analyze accidents/incidents likely to have significant consequences. Thoroughly investigate and report on all HSE incidents and near-misses likely to have significant actual or potential consequences. Set improvement targets, mediating, evaluating and reporting as necessary.
