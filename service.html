Our Professional Services

GROUP AGECOSCO provides comprehensive maritime inspection and cargo survey services to ensure the safety, quality, and compliance of your maritime operations across West Africa.

Tally Inspections & Supervisions

Professional cargo counting, condition assessment, and supervision services including charter-party disputes, bunker disputes, and stevedore operations management.

  • Cargo counting and tallying
  • Loading/discharge supervision
  • Charter-party dispute resolution
Learn More

Marine Surveys & Average Adjustments

Comprehensive marine surveys, transport recommendations, and supervision of project cargoes including road surveys and average adjustment services.

  • Vessel condition surveys
  • Cargo damage assessments
  • Average adjustment calculations
Learn More

Crisis Control & Emergency Response

Rapid response crisis control interventions with dedicated professionals providing expert emergency maritime assistance and incident management.

  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Incident investigation
  • Crisis management support
Learn More

Risk Management & Prevention

Specialized risk management services focusing on pest prevention and control for vessels, cargo, port facilities, and offshore platforms.

  • Risk assessment and analysis
  • Pest prevention programs
  • Safety compliance audits
